SWOT Analysis
Internal Strengths Weaknesses
  • Responsive Design
    • Is able to open up and fit different screen sizes.
    • Can capatilize on this to increase traffic from people who shop on mobile devices.
  • Good Color Choices and Theme
    • Has a cityscape theme and logo that works well
    • Could capatilize of this by incorporating more throughout the design of the website.
  • Has a Nice Nav Bar.
    • Has a good nav bar that makes finding products easy.
    • It could update some of the elements of the nav bar such as the font.
  • Overall Layout
    • Looks very cluttered
    • Could eliminate some of the items and just highlight the main ones.
  • Slideshow Page
    • The slideshow on the main page is complicated and hard to look at.
    • The slideshow could either be redesigned to look simpler or taken out altogether.
  • Outdated Look
    • Overall the look of the page seems dated
    • The design of the page could be incorporating elements of a modern e-commerce website.
External Opportunities Threats
  • Trend
    • Electric scooters and skateboards are a trendy item that a lot of people enjoy.
    • Urban scooters can commit to the trend of electric scooters and scooters to boost sales.
  • Street Scooters (City)
    • Can capatilize on the fact that many peoples use electric scooters to get around major cities.
    • Also fits with their cityscape theme.
  • Mobility Scooters
    • Sells medical mobility scooters
    • Can use this as an opportunity to highlight these kinds of scooters for those who need them.
  • High Prices
    • A lot of these scooters are in the thousands price range.
    • Some of the more entry level models could be highlighted to customers.
  • Competition
    • Could run into competition in the same space that hurts sales.
    • Could prioritize the items that sell the most and feature them for customers
  • Inventory Size
    • Has a high inventory size that might make keeping a consistent inventory difficult.
    • Could stop selling a few of the items that don't sell well rather than just showing them as out of stock.
